FAQs

Is SSL Encryption included on my website?

Full SSL Encryption is included in all of our packages so that your website visitors can feel confident when using your website.

Are the created websites responsive?

Yes, all created websites are automatically responsive (RWD) and adapt to various screen sizes.

Are the websites of good quality

Yes, we focus on SEO optimization, and Web Vitals metrics are taken into account when calculating search engine rankings. The websites achieve excellent results in Google Lighthouse tests, which positively impacts their search engine ranking.

Does the website have an SSL certificate?

Yes, all websites have an SSL certificate. An SSL certificate is a digital certificate that authenticates a website's identity and enables an encrypted connection. You will get HTTPS with every plan.

Are the websites optimized for Google?

Yes, we take care of all elements important for SEO, such as URLs, meta tags, alt attributes, internal links, headers, and keywords. Additionally, we add structured data tailored to your business.

I already have my own domain, can it be connected?

Yes, if you have your own domain registered with another registrar, we can connect it to the website being built.

Are the websites created in WordPress?

No, these are not WordPress websites. We build and host the websites on our Najszybsza platform. We use our own website builder. Each website is built from scratch, so it's unique.

How much does an online store cost?

The cost of creating an online store can vary significantly. It depends on the complexity of the chosen functionality, design, and integrations. Therefore, to clarify the costs, please contact our specialists.
