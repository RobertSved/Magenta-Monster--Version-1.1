About

Sumptuous yielding comfort with unmatched views

The LakeHouse is the perfect romantic getaway in Lake District. You will be comfortable in this spacious and unique space as it is one of the largest in the area. It has been recently renovated with all mod cons and includes a double shower and a superking size beds. Both free WiFi and parking on-site are available at the apartment free of charge. It is within walking distance to the numerous restaurants and bars.

The property is open plan with an industrial design. We personally designed and created a unique space unlike any other apartments in the area.

The Lake District has no shortage of outdoor activities. From kayaking and fishing to mountain hiking, your choice of activities is limitless. Rent canoes, kayaks or paddleboards and take to water. Or if you want a cycling activity use your time and go into the unknown. All in one place.
